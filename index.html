The Journey

From Product Owner to the Leader Everyone Turns To

A year of relentless growth — building trust, scaling teams, driving revenue, and becoming the person people come to when they need a solution.

March 10, 2025

Joined Capco

Started at Capco as an AI Product Owner, ready to make an impact from day one.

End of March 2025

Joined Jefferies as AI Intake Consultant

Hit the ground running on the Jefferies account — designed the AI intake process from scratch, led use-case guided discovery sessions, and built AI solution prototype documentation. Quickly became the person the team relied on to turn ambiguous AI requests into structured, actionable plans.

Mid 2025

Founded the US GenAI Demo Day

Identified a gap in how AI work was being showcased internally and created the US GenAI Demo Day (bi-weekly). What started as a technical forum quickly evolved into an internal sales tool — converting demo assets into repeatable, sales-ready collateral that accelerates pursuits.

Mid–Late 2025

Demo Day Goes Global & Lab Doubles

Within a couple of months, the US Demo Day scaled into a Global Demo Day — coordinating across UK, Canada, Tax, Switzerland/Germany, Singapore, and India. Simultaneously, the US GenAI Lab membership doubled as demand surged, requiring new governance, guidelines, and onboarding processes that Jessie helped establish.

Late 2025

Lab Triples to 62 Members & Global AI Product Lead

The GenAI Lab grew to 3× its original size (15 → 62 members, 28 active standup). Jessie stepped up as AI Product Lead on the US side for the Global AI Lab, running critical operating forums and governance alongside senior leadership. She became the reliable leader teams turned to for direction, alignment, and problem-solving.

Late 2025

Appointed JefAI Product Lead

Promoted from intake consultant to full Product Lead for Jefferies' AI platform. Now owning the roadmap, stakeholder management across C-level and ED-level, and programme delivery for ~26 engineers. Also began identifying commercial opportunities within Jefferies, supporting GTM & RFP efforts, and staying deeply curious about the sales pipeline — understanding which clients, which solutions, and what the revenue picture looks like for the AI team.

Early January 2026

Supporting Canada & Cross-Region Collaboration

Stepped in to support the Canada team for RFP and Demo preparation, getting them up to speed on AI capabilities and helping position Capco's AI story for new pursuits — the kind of cross-region support that happens when you're the go-to person for AI solutions.

January 2026

Founded AI PO/PM Capability & Thought Leadership

Started building Capco's first AI Product Owner/Product Manager group (1 → 4 members), establishing training and onboarding so juniors can run delivery autonomously. Simultaneously invited as a panelist at the AI Collective × La Crème de la STEM event in Manhattan (Jan 28) alongside Google and Neon&Slate — representing Capco on the global AI stage. Invited 3+ times to speak as AI product lead and expert on Jefferies AI Ambassadors calls, providing training and tailored solutions for enterprise-wide and department-specific AI platforms.

February 2026

AI × Cyber Partnership & L&D Initiative

Launched an official AI × Cyber collaboration with the Cyber team, bridging two critical domains to productise marketable AI security solutions. Also invited to join the Lunch & Learn initiative for the AI Infusion team, expanding Jessie's role as a trusted voice for AI education and enablement across the firm.

March 2026

Working Directly with COO on AI Enablement

Now working directly with the COO to kick off a firm-wide AI enablement initiative — an educational programme encouraging domains to submit AI use cases through a strategy of forward engineering to accelerate how AI solutions get built and deployed. This is leadership beyond product: it's shaping how Capco thinks about AI at an organisational level.
